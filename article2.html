How do you eat an elephant?

And so it begins.

It’s so discouraging to start something new sometimes. It can feel like taking on Mount Everest or an impossible marathon. Like how am I ever gunna get to the top or finish the damn thing when I’ve never done it before?

I’ve asked myself that question for about a year and now I’m all questioned out. I’ve worked myself to exhaustion by remaining anxious and frozen, I might as well give doing something a try.

One of the things I’m going to start doing is setting up a 30 minutes timer, twice a day, to do a bit of freeCodeCamp. If I want to keep going after the 30 minutes are done(like it happened today) that’s well and good. And if I want to stop at the 30 minutes mark, equally amazing.

I’ve just finished the HTML part, the next step is copying my articles from Medium to a self made HTML only website. I’m kinda excited. I’m not going to move onto CSS until my HTML site is deployed and ready.

How do you eat an elephant? One bite at a time.
